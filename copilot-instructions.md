# Unseenleads Agency Website Brief

This document outlines the requirements for the Unseenleads agency website, to be built using Next.js and Tailwind CSS. The goal is to create a website that is bold, simple, and elegant, reflecting the brand's commitment to high-converting solutions for local service businesses.

---

## 1. Agency Overview

- **Name:** Unseenleads
- **Mission:** An agency specializing in building high-converting websites and boosting the likelihood of businesses being found on search engines and social media.
- **Focus:** Primarily serves local-based service businesses.

---

## 2. Brand Identity

- **Overall Mood:** Bold, simple, and elegant. The design should feel premium, professional, and trustworthy, yet approachable for local businesses.
- **Typography:**
  - **Headings:** Jakarta Sans
  - **Body Text:** Geist
- **Colors:** Neutral colors. Based on the provided hero section, the primary palette should revolve around:
  - **Background:** Clean whites or very light greys.
  - **Text:** Strong blacks or very dark greys for high contrast.
  - **Accents:** Subtle shades of grey for secondary elements (e.g., button backgrounds, borders, social icons) to maintain elegance without overwhelming the neutral palette.

---

## 3. Design Principles (Inspired by Hero Section)

The website's design should emulate the mood and aesthetic of the provided hero section:

- **Minimalist & Clean Layout:** Prioritize clear space and avoid clutter. Every element should serve a purpose.
- **Bold Typography:** Headings should be prominent and impactful, conveying confidence and directness.
- **High Contrast:** Ensure excellent readability with strong contrast between text and background.
- **Structured Content:** Use clear visual hierarchy to guide the user's eye. Information should be easy to digest.
- **Subtle Visual Cues:** Incorporate subtle design elements like rounded corners on buttons and input fields, and clean, modern icons.
- **Professionalism:** The overall aesthetic should communicate expertise and reliability.
- **Direct Call to Action (CTA):** Prominent and clear CTAs, similar to the "Free Consult" button, should be strategically placed.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure good contrast and readable font sizes across all devices.
- **Responsiveness:** The design must be fully responsive and look excellent on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices without horizontal scrolling.

---

## 4. Technical Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (app router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **language:** JavaScript

---

## 5. Website Sections (Proposed)

The website should include, but not be limited to, the following sections:

- **Hero Section:** (As designed, capturing "Rule Your Local Market" with a clear CTA and contact icons).
- **Services:** Detailed explanation of website building, SEO, and social media visibility services.
- **About Us:** Introduce the agency, its values, and its approach.
- **Portfolio/Case Studies:** Showcase successful projects (even if mock data initially).
- **Testimonials:** Social proof from satisfied clients.
- **Contact:** A form for inquiries, along with contact details and social media links.
- **Blog (Optional but Recommended):** For thought leadership and SEO.

---

## 6. General Instructions

- **Prioritize Performance:** Ensure fast loading times and smooth transitions.
- **SEO Best Practices:** Implement semantic HTML, meta tags, and structured data where appropriate.
- **User Experience (UX):** Focus on intuitive navigation and a seamless user journey.
- **Code Quality:** Generate clean, well-commented, and maintainable code.
- **Iterative Development:** Be prepared for feedback and iterative refinements.
